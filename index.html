RavenClaw Robotics FLL 2025–2026

Welcome to the 2025–2026 FLL Season

This year's theme is UNEARTHED — and it's all about exploring what's hidden underground, discovering hidden artifacts or treasure. Everyone can have fun if you follow the core values.

🤝 Core Values

Teamwork

We work together, split tasks, and help each other when stuff breaks (which it will).

Innovation

We come up with weird ideas and test them until something actually works.

Discovery

We learn new things—sometimes by accident, sometimes on purpose.

Inclusion

Everyone gets a say, and everyone gets to try stuff. No one's left out.

Impact

We want our work to matter—to our team, our school, and maybe even the world.

Fun

We laugh, we build, we mess up, and we keep going. That's the whole point.

Real World Challenges

How can robots help archaeologists uncover fragile artifacts without damaging them?

Can we detect underground hazards like sinkholes before they collapse and destroy stuff?

What kind of tech can help scientists study underground ecosystems without messing them up?

These are just a few examples of what teams might explore this season. It's all about solving problems that happen below the surface.

🧪 Innovation Project

Every team picks a real underground-related problem and tries to solve it. You research it, brainstorm ideas, and come up with something smart. Then you share it with people who know the field—like engineers, scientists, or even your school community.

It could be about preventing sinkholes, improving tunnel safety, or helping archaeologists. Whatever you choose, make it useful and creative.

🤖 Robot Game Missions

Teams build and code autonomous robots to complete missions on the UNEARTHED game field. These missions might include brushing off artifacts, navigating tunnels, or detecting underground hazards.

You can't touch the robot during the run, so it needs to be smart and precise. Strategy matters. Watch the full breakdown in the Robot Game Missions video.

📅 Season Timeline

Here's a rough idea of how the season usually goes:

  • August: Challenge kickoff and mission release
  • September–October: Team formation, robot building, and project research
  • November–January: Qualifiers and presentations
  • February–March: Regionals and championships (if you qualify!)
